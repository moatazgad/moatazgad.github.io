About Me

I'm a Cloud & DevOps consultant who helps teams ship faster and more reliably. I design infrastructure, build CI/CD pipelines, and automate the things that slow people down.

My day-to-day involves working with AWS, Azure, Terraform, Kubernetes, and GitHub Actions — turning manual processes into repeatable, scalable systems. I focus on making cloud architecture simple, secure, and cost-effective.

I'm also exploring how AI fits into the DevOps workflow — from intelligent monitoring and incident response to using LLMs for infrastructure-as-code generation and documentation.

Whether it's migrating workloads to the cloud, setting up observability, or helping a team adopt GitOps, I enjoy solving the problems that sit between code and production.
